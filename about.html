I'm Shilpa, a seventeen-year-old writer and graphic designer. I hail from College Station, Texas; I'm currently a freshman at the University of Pennsylvania, where I study linguistics and computer science. My greatest loves are books, the French Revolution, and droids (fictional or otherwise).

I've been producing art for as long as I can remember, but I only realized how much I loved graphic design when my dad came home one day with a Wacom tablet. It came with a free copy of Photoshop Elements. The rest, as they say, is history.

I honed my Adobe Creative Suite skills throughout high school, where I wrote, designed, and photographed for the school newspaper. During this time, I began designing T-shirts -- both for sale (here) and for commission. After noticing the severe lack of simple, clean themes on several blogging platforms, I began designing those, too.
